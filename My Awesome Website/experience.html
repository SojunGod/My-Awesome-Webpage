Background

I began my meditation journey when I was 15 years old. When I was old enough to go to college I decided to study Buddhist philosophy under Victor Sogen Hori at McGill University in Montreal, Canada. I continued my studies, this time as a fully ordained Zen monk at Daitokuji Temple where I stayed for nine years. Finally, I decided that I was more use outside of the temple so I moved back to the US in 2009. Since then I have taught yoga, and meditation, engaged in social activism, and taught school to under-served students in rural Vermont, then to students with trauma backgrounds in Burlington area.
